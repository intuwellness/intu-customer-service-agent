# INTU Wellness — Policies Reference

## Shipping Policy
Source: https://intuwellness.com/shipping/

**Delivery coverage:** Australia, UK, Ireland, USA, Asia, Europe Zone 1

**Australian shipping costs:**
- Standard: $7 flat rate
- Express: $10 flat rate

**International shipping:** Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. DDU (Delivery Duty Unpaid) — customer is responsible for import duties, customs fees, and local taxes on arrival.

**Processing & dispatch:**
- Orders processed within 1–2 business days (Monday–Friday)
- Same-day dispatch available for orders placed before 10:30 AM
- Standard Australian delivery: 3–8 business days (typically 3–5)
- Express Australian delivery: 1–3 business days

**Tracking:**
- Confirmation email sent from hello@intuwellness.com with tracking details
- Tracking information can take up to 48 hours to update after dispatch
- Tracking provided by third parties; INTU assumes no responsibility for third-party tracking errors
- Customers should include a phone number at checkout for SMS delivery updates

## Refund & Returns Policy
Source: https://intuwellness.com/online-terms-of-use/

## Subscription Policy

**Known portal issue:** The subscription portal has a known bug where cancellations show as "pending" indefinitely. Always cancel manually when a customer requests cancellation — do not ask them to try again.

**Subscription changes:** The portal UX is difficult for customers. Handle frequency changes manually and confirm the updated next dispatch date clearly.

**Unexpected charges:** Check whether a dormant subscription reactivated. Confirm with Natalie whether the order has shipped before requesting a return. If the charge cannot be explained, escalate to Natalie.
